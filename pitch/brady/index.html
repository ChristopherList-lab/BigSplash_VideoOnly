Only One

Country music museum in Texas

US-87, US-377, and US-190 all converge in Brady. Six thousand cars a day move through downtown between San Angelo, Fredericksburg, and the Hill Country. Most don't stop. They tank up and keep moving.

The frac-sand jobs left between 2018 and 2019. Pioneer Sands shut at the end of 2019. Median household income dropped 12% the next year. The mayor at the time named tourism as the rebrand strategy out loud. The chamber is acting on that call.

Brady owns two things no peer town can copy. The World Championship BBQ Goat Cook-Off, 51 years running, 200+ teams, 5,000 pounds of goat smoked every Labor Day. The Heart of Texas Country Music Museum, the only Texas museum dedicated to traditional country, run by Tracy Pitcox who also runs the festival every March. Fredericksburg has wine. Llano has Cooper's. Brady has goats and country music.

The chamber doesn't own a film about either one. visitbrady.com runs three short clips and a templated tour. There is no native YouTube channel. There is no Goat Cook-Off recap reel. Daniel Mendoza directs both the chamber and the EDC, so a single conversation reaches both budgets.

Whoever puts Brady's two anchors on film first becomes the chamber's main reel for the next decade.

Why Now

Two windows open at the same time.

Window One

One Desk. Two Budgets.

Daniel Mendoza is both Chamber Executive Director and Brady EDC Director. The chamber retainer track and the EDC tourism track route through the same staff lead. One conversation, two funding paths.

The City of Brady levies a Hotel Occupancy Tax with no published competitive grant portal. HOT awards route through City Council and staff request. Per Texas Tax Code, tourism video is an explicit eligible use. EDC Type A and Type B funds cover business attraction and infrastructure. Three funding lanes, all reachable from one inbox.

Window Two

Two Anchors No Peer Town Can Copy.

The Goat Cook-Off turns 52 next Labor Day. 200+ teams, 5,000 pounds of goat, Texas Legislature recognition, statewide press. The Heart of Texas Country Music Museum is the only one in Texas. Tracy Pitcox runs both the museum and the festival every March.

The chamber currently owns zero film about either anchor. visitbrady.com runs a templated elocallink.tv tour. No YouTube channel. The category is wide open and the assets are uncontested.
