Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked.

Common questions about pricing, timelines, and how we work. Don't see yours? Reach out.

How much does a video ad cost?

add

Video ads typically run $2,500 to $8,000 per ad depending on scope, talent, and locations. Multi-ad packages and retainers lower the per-ad cost. Most service businesses start with a 3-ad test package so we can compare what actually converts before scaling spend.

How long does a typical project take?

add

Short-form social ads take 2 to 3 weeks from kickoff to delivery. Brand films and commercials run 4 to 8 weeks depending on complexity. Discovery calls happen within 1 business day of your inquiry.

Do you work outside Georgetown or Austin?

add

Yes. We're based in Georgetown, TX and shoot throughout the Austin metro. We also travel nationwide for the right project. Remote strategy and content planning engagements happen on Zoom.

What industries do you specialize in?

add

Service businesses that rely on trust and local reputation: chiropractors, roofers, contractors, auto, real estate, medical practices, and consultants. Any business where the content has to pay for itself through leads and calls.

Do you handle paid media and ad management?

add

Yes. Our Content Strategy service covers Meta and TikTok ad management, audience targeting, creative testing, and performance reporting. We can build the content and run the ads, or just build the creative for your existing team.

What's included in a video ad package?

add

Full pipeline: strategy, scriptwriting, location scouting, shoot day with our crew, editing, color, sound design, motion graphics where needed, and multiple cutdowns per ad (9:16, 1:1, 16:9).

Can I buy a one-off video?

add

Yes. Event recaps, testimonial videos, product walkthroughs, and stand-alone brand pieces are all available. Service businesses still get our full production team, just scoped to a single deliverable.

How do we get started?

add

Book a discovery call or send us a message through the site. We ask about your business, goals, and where your current content is missing. You'll hear back within one business day.
