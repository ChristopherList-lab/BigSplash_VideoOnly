What this is: a menu of image prompts written for Gemini 3 Pro Image ("Nano Banana 2"). Every prompt preserves the brand: muted warm palette, film-grain 35mm, candid not glam, mid-life real-woman energy. Paste them verbatim — the order of words matters.

House rules · every prompt follows these

  • Palette: sage, dusty rose, warm taupe, soft tan, cream. Never primary colors. Never saturated.
  • Light: natural window light or golden hour. No studio flash. No ring-light beauty look.
  • Texture: film grain, Kodak Portra 400 / Cinestill 800T references. No plastic AI skin.
  • Subject: mid-life woman 40s, brown hair, natural beauty — never over-glammed.
  • Lens: 50mm f/1.8 default — soft background, crisp subject.
  • Always append: no text, no watermark, no logos, clean background.

06 · Phone-to-editorial transform

The most powerful use. She snaps a phone photo → upload + paste this prompt → gets a grid-ready editorial version back.

Workflow Transform a phone photo

Upload photo + paste prompt
Transform the attached phone photo of the subject into a film-grain 35mm editorial photograph. Keep the subject's face, body, and clothing exactly as they are. Re-light with soft warm window light from the left, add subtle Kodak Portra 400 color grading, add gentle film grain, desaturate slightly, warm up the whites, deepen the shadows. Clean up any digital artifacts. Muted warm palette — sage, cream, taupe, soft gold. No added text, no watermark. Preserve the authentic expression and candid feel.
