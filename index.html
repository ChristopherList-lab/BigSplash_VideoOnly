FAQ · 10

ANSWERS,
UPFRONT.

The things everyone asks before the kickoff call. For the full list, head to the FAQ page.

How much does a campaign cost?

add
Video ads start at $1,200 per creative. Brand films start at $3,500. Ongoing short-form retainers start at $800/month. Every quote is locked before we shoot — no surprise invoices.

How fast can we launch?

add
Standard timeline is 2–3 weeks from kickoff to live campaign. Rush jobs possible — ask on the call.

Do you run the ads, or just make them?

add
Both. We can hand off final assets to your media buyer, or handle Meta + TikTok spend end-to-end with weekly reporting. Most service-biz clients go with the second option.

Will you travel outside Georgetown?

add
Yes — we cover all of Central Texas (Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, San Antonio) at no travel surcharge. Farther out, ask on the call.
